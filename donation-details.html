The educational opportunities available to children in Sri Lanka are limited due to poverty, inequality and lack of funding. Donations from individuals can provide a much-needed boost to education programs that help expand the possibilities for these young learners. Every dollar you donate gives an opportunity for a child to pursue their dreams of going on with formal schooling or any type of Early Childhood Development (ECD) initiatives like nutrition programmes designed specifically for preschoolers. Your donation would also go a long way towards helping schools increase access to technology tools, books and teaching materials; develop curriculums tailored for success; support language instruction programming; and train future teachers through apprenticeship programs.

You're helping to change lives and create a brighter future for generations of children living in this beautiful island nation.

Your donations will go directly towards providing educational opportunities that support the growth and development of young minds. Educational programs include - but are not limited to – basic literacy skills; mathematics training; computer science tutorials; English language instruction; physical health classes such as yoga and Pilates; creative art forms such as music, theatre, dance and martial arts); civic engagement initiatives focused on environmental consciousness and youth leadership.
